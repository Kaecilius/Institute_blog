The four primary Fallout 4 factions are each aligned toward different goals and interests. The Minutemen seek to promote the development of Settements ad stability across the Commonwealth. The Railroad seek to help the Synths created by The Institute to escape and enjoy an independent existence. The Brotherhood of Steel hope to create a stable, militaristic, and well-armed haven for human civilization. The Institute's mysterious motives involve the development and exploitation of synthetic life.
